Dr. Kishor R. Kolhe

Welcome to my home page!

I am Dr. Kishor Kolhe, Associate Professor, in the School of Computer Engineering and Technology at MIT World Peace University, Pune. My research interest primarily involves Network and Security, Software Engineering and Internet of Things. I always believe in improving education by investigating innovative pedagogies, interactive teaching practice with modern technology.

I have more than 24 years of teaching and industry experience also held various administrative positions like HOD, System In-charge, CEO (Examinations) etc. I am a Life Member of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), Computer Society of India (CSI) and Fellow life member of Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE). I have served as the organizing Co-Chair for National Conference held at MIT Campus. Besides this, organized numerous Faculty Development Programs, Seminars and Workshops for undergraduates and postgraduate students. My published work to date, (more than 50) in reputed Conferences and Journals cover the theoretical and experimental work in the related domain.

Outside of work, I enjoy swimming, running and traveling. Explore my home page to know more about me and my work.

Professional Societies Engagements

Dr. Kishor R. Kolhe is also a member of many professional societies. Lets have a glance at them.

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE)

Life Member

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

As a part of engagements, I have attended 3 ISTE organized faculty development programs.

Computer Society of India (CSI)

Life Member

Computer Society of India (CSI) is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It was started on 6 March 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals.

I have attended and Organized Technical development programs for faculty and students which were supported by CSI Pune chapter.

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE)

Life Fellow Member

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) is India's leading recognized professional society devoted to the advancement of Science and Technology of Electronics,Telecommunication & IT. Founded in 1953. The IETE focuses on advancement of the Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, Computers, Information Technology and related areas. Towards this end the Institution promotes and conducts basic engineering and continuing technical education programs for human resource development.

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE)

Member

IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better tomorrow through highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities. IEEE is the trusted “voice” for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

I have attended various IEEE conferences in INDIA. Also engaged in attending and organizing IEEE affiliated technical programs for competency.
